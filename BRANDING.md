# BayScan Branding

## Brand Identity

**Name:** BayScan
**Tagline:** Smart Fishing Forecasts for Mobile Bay
**Domain:** bayscan.app
**Primary Color:** Ocean Blue (#2c5364)
**Accent Color:** Sunset Orange (#ff6b35)

## Logo Concept

The BayScan logo could incorporate:
- A bay/water wave motif
- Radar/scan lines suggesting intelligence/analysis
- Fish silhouette
- Minimalist, modern design

## Brand Voice

- **Knowledgeable but approachable** - Like a seasoned fishing buddy
- **Data-driven** - Scientific approach to fishing
- **Local** - Specific to Mobile Bay and Gulf Coast
- **Helpful** - Focused on making fishing more successful

## Key Messages

1. **Smart predictions based on real science** - Not just guessing, using NOAA data and proven fishing patterns
2. **Local expertise** - Built specifically for Mobile Bay species and conditions
3. **Save time** - Know the best times to fish before you head out
4. **Increase success** - Target the right species at the right time

## Visual Style

- **Clean, modern interface** - Easy to read on phone while on the water
- **Data visualization** - Charts and graphs that tell the story at a glance
- **Mobile-first** - Designed for the dock, boat, or beach
- **Color-coded alerts** - Quick visual cues for hot fishing windows

## Use Cases

### Primary Users
- **Dock fishermen** on Mobile Bay
- **Kayak anglers** planning trips
- **Weekend warriors** maximizing limited fishing time
- **Serious anglers** looking for competitive edges

### Key Features They Love
- Real-time tide predictions
- Species-specific bite windows
- Weather integration
- Alert notifications for hot conditions

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Hyper-local** - Specific to Mobile Bay, not generic
2. **Species-specific** - 8 different species with custom logic
3. **Free & open** - No subscription fees
4. **Privacy-focused** - Your data stays yours
5. **Always available** - Self-hosted or cloud option

## Future Branding Opportunities

- BayScan mobile app
- BayScan for other bays (Tampa Bay, Galveston Bay, etc.)
- BayScan Pro (premium features)
- BayScan Community (catch logging, local reports)
- Branded merchandise for supporters
